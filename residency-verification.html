Residency Verification

Verification of residents graduated from the following residency programs may be obtained by contacting the residency coordinator listed below:

  • Yale Podiatric Medicine and Surgery Residency with added credential in Reconstructive Rearfoot/Ankle Surgery (PMSR/RRA)
  • Yale/VACT Podiatric Medicine and Surgery Residency -36 (PMS-36)
  • Yale/VACT Podiatric Surgery Residency -24 (PSR-24)
  • Yale/VACT Podiatric Surgery Residency -12 (PSR-12)
  • Yale/VACT Primary Podiatric Medicine Residency (PPMR)
  • Yale/VACT Podiatric Orthopedic Residency -24 (POR)
  • West Haven VA-Podiatric Surgery Residency

Please note: Bridgeport Hospital is a stand-alone community hospital in the YNH Health System. Bridgeport Hospital podiatry residents do not rotate through Yale New Haven Medical Center in New Haven and verification of Bridgeport Hospital Podiatry residents is NOT done through Yale Medical School or Yale New Haven Medical Center, rather it comes directly from Bridgeport Hospital.
